Case study · SaaS · CRO

How 26 Vantage landing page tests added $1.4M in annual recurring revenue

Vantage was converting 2.1% of trial starts to paid. Good traffic, weak close rate. We ran a structured testing program — one hypothesis per week, every test documented. The headline winner: reframing "Start free trial" as "Start your commitment-free pilot." Small sentence, big consequence. Over three quarters, trial-to-paid conversion hit 3.4%.
