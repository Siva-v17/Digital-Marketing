Our story

Founded on a simple frustration.

In 2016, our founders were running marketing for three different startups and kept hitting the same wall: agencies that reported on vanity metrics while revenue stayed flat. So they built the agency they wished they could hire — one obsessed with the metrics that actually matter to a board.

Today Stackly is a team of 38 strategists, designers, analysts, and media buyers across New York and Lisbon, working with brands from seed-stage startups to category leaders. Every engagement starts the same way: we read your P&L before we touch your ad account.

We're not the biggest agency. We're not trying to be. We take on a limited number of clients each quarter to make sure every account is fully resourced — not handed to a junior team after the pitch.

What we believe

Four principles that shape every engagement.

01

Revenue is the only metric

Impressions and clicks are inputs. We report, plan, and get paid against pipeline and revenue. If it doesn't touch the top line, we'll tell you.

02

Strategy before software

Tools change every year. We start with positioning and unit economics, then pick the stack to match. The strategy survives platform changes.

03

Transparent, always

You see the same dashboard we do. No black boxes, no inflated channel attribution. If a channel isn't working, you'll hear it from us first.

04

Built to be fired

No lock-in contracts disguised as "strategy." We earn the renewal every quarter, on results. That keeps us honest in ways a contract never could.

Milestones

A decade, condensed.

2016

Stackly is founded

Three people, one shared office in Brooklyn, and a single retainer client in fintech. Our first dashboard was a Google Sheet. We are not ashamed.

2018

Paid media desk launches

We bring media buying in-house after watching too many handoffs slow campaigns down. A dedicated team means no more "we sent the brief to the partner" excuses.

2021

100th client milestone & Lisbon opens

We hit our 100th client engagement and immediately opened a second studio in Lisbon. European time zones, same standards as New York.

2023

Proprietary reporting suite

Launched our live client dashboard, replacing static monthly PDF reports for good. Every client now sees their numbers update in real time — same data we use to make decisions.

2026

240+ brands scaled

Now a team of 38 across two continents, managing $86M in annual ad spend. Still taking on fewer clients than we could, on purpose.
